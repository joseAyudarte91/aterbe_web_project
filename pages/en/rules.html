Hotel policies

We require all the customers to follow some basic rules:

  • A kid under the age of 3 is allowed to stay for free using the existing beds
  • No pets are allowed

Reservation policies

  • In order to make a reservation customers are required to pay the 25% of the total stay as a deposit
  • The rest of the payment is made after the check-in
  • Only rooms are rented

Cancelation policies

  • The deposit is not refundable, unless special conditions are met

The reservation policies are subjected to changes in special dates, such as holidays, summer season and long weekends.
