Services

Room cleaning and breakfast are included with the stay. We also offer spa sessions and a mini-bar with self-service, which are not included with the stay.

The breakfast

As a general policy, the breakfast is served between 9:30am and 12:00am. Otherwise, customers are allowed to prepare the breakfast themselves at any time as a buffet service. The next good-quality products are offered in the breakfast:

  • Coffe and milk
  • Home-made bread and toasts
  • Yoghurts
  • Fruits and a variety of juices
  • Natural orange juice
  • Bakery
  • Tea
  • Cola Cao

There is no way to assure the availability of all the mentioned products.

Spa services

We offer private spa sessions for 30 euros per hour for 2 people, where you can use the next services:

  • Finnish Sauna
  • Turkish Bath
  • Relax area with deck-chairs
  • Massage room
  • Changing room and toilet

In order to use these services you need to notify us some time before. You can check the benefits and things to take care about when using a sauna in the next link: benefits and risks of saunas.

Minibar

Self-service exclusive for customers. Each consumed product must appear in a notepad with the number of the room where the customer stays in. The product list with its related price is shown in the minibar.

We have several domestic appliances: coffe maker, microwave, fridge and freezer. The fridge can be used by customers to store their food and drinks under request.

Additional equipment

Besides the services described above, we also have:

  • Private parking
  • Terrace, garden and cellar
  • Living room with a wood stove
  • Free internet available (Wi-Fi)
  • Crystal porch with hammocks
  • Barbaque in the summer
  • Board games
  • Payment via credit card

The first-aid kit is located at the hall. There you will have access to hotlines and the first-aid manual in case you need them.
